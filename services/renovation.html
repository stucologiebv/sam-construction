Renovation

Transforming Spaces, Preserving Character

Luxembourg's architectural heritage spans centuries — from historic farmhouses in the Ardennes to early 20th-century townhouses in Luxembourg City. At SAM Construction, we approach every renovation with deep respect for the existing structure while bringing it into full compliance with modern standards of comfort, safety and energy efficiency.

Whether you are modernising a family home, converting a commercial property or restoring a listed building, our team brings the technical skill and sensitivity that complex renovation projects demand.

  • Kitchen and bathroom remodelling
  • Roof replacement and attic conversions
  • Window and door upgrades (triple-glazed, energy-rated)
  • Electrical and plumbing system modernisation

Working Within Luxembourg Regulations

Renovation in Luxembourg requires careful navigation of building codes, commune-specific rules and, in many cases, heritage protection requirements. We manage the entire permitting process, liaising with the commune, the Service des Sites et Monuments Nationaux where applicable, and all relevant technical inspectors.

For energy renovations, we help clients take advantage of Luxembourg's PRIMe House subsidies and other government incentive programmes designed to encourage improvements in energy performance.

Phased Renovations

We understand that not every renovation can happen at once. Our team is experienced in planning phased renovation programmes that allow you to spread the work — and the investment — over time, while ensuring each phase integrates seamlessly with the next. Where possible, we coordinate works to allow continued occupation of the property, minimising disruption to daily life.

Transparent Process

Every renovation begins with a detailed survey and assessment. We provide comprehensive cost estimates broken down by trade and phase, so you always know exactly where your investment is going. Regular progress updates, on-site meetings and a dedicated project manager keep everything on track.
