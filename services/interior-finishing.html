Interior Finishing

The details that define a space — precision plastering, flooring, bespoke millwork and artisan finishes.

Where Craftsmanship Meets Design

Interior finishing is where a building becomes a home, an office becomes a workspace, and raw construction becomes refined architecture. At SAM Construction, we treat the finishing phase with the same rigour and attention that we bring to structural works — because the details are what people live with every day.

Our finishing teams are skilled craftspeople who take pride in delivering flawless results. From the first coat of plaster to the final coat of paint, from precisely laid tiles to custom-built cabinetry, every element is executed to the highest standard.

Our Interior Finishing Services

  • Plastering — machine-applied and hand-finished to perfectly smooth or textured surfaces
  • Painting and decorative wall treatments
  • Tiling — floor and wall, including large-format porcelain and natural stone
  • Hardwood, engineered wood and laminate flooring installation
  • Custom joinery — fitted wardrobes, shelving, kitchen cabinets and bespoke millwork
  • Suspended ceilings and acoustic treatments
  • Interior door installation and hardware fitting
  • Skirting boards, architraves and decorative mouldings

Material Selection

We work with a curated network of Luxembourg and European suppliers to source materials that match your vision and budget. Whether you prefer the warmth of natural oak, the contemporary elegance of polished concrete, or the timeless appeal of natural stone, we source and install with equal expertise.

All materials used in our interior works meet European standards for indoor air quality, fire resistance and durability. We prioritise low-VOC paints, sustainably sourced timbers and materials with verified environmental product declarations (EPDs).

Coordination with Other Trades

Interior finishing requires precise coordination with electrical, plumbing and HVAC trades. Our project managers ensure that all concealed services are properly installed and tested before finishing works begin, preventing costly rework and ensuring clean, seamless results.

Attention to Detail

We conduct thorough quality inspections at every stage of the finishing process. Before handover, a comprehensive snagging walk-through identifies any imperfections for correction. Our clients receive a space that is truly move-in ready, with every surface, fitting and finish meeting our exacting standards.
