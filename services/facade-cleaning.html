Façade Cleaning

Restoring the beauty of building exteriors with specialist cleaning, pressure washing and protective treatments for lasting results.

Restoring First Impressions

A building's façade is its public face — the first thing visitors see and the lasting impression it makes on the streetscape. Over time, pollution, biological growth, weathering and mineral deposits can dull even the finest exteriors. SAM Construction's façade cleaning service restores that original beauty, protecting your investment and maintaining your property's value.

Operating across Luxembourg, we clean all types of exterior surfaces — from traditional sandstone and rendered walls to modern glass and aluminium cladding systems. Every project begins with a thorough assessment to identify the right cleaning method for the specific substrate and type of soiling.

Our Façade Cleaning Services

  • High-pressure and low-pressure water cleaning
  • Steam cleaning for delicate surfaces
  • Chemical cleaning for stubborn stains and biological growth
  • Sand and soda blasting (gentle abrasive methods)
  • Graffiti removal
  • Anti-graffiti protective coatings
  • Hydrophobic (water-repellent) treatments
  • Window and glass façade cleaning

Protecting Heritage Surfaces

Luxembourg's architectural heritage includes many buildings with natural stone façades — Luxembourg sandstone, Jaumont limestone and slate are common. These materials require specialist cleaning techniques that remove soiling without damaging the stone surface or its natural patina. We use appropriate methods such as low-pressure nebulised water, gentle chemical poultices and micro-abrasive techniques to achieve excellent results without harm.

For buildings under the protection of the Service des Sites et Monuments Nationaux, we coordinate our approach with the relevant authorities to ensure compliance with heritage preservation requirements.

Protective Treatments

Cleaning is only half the story. We apply advanced protective treatments that extend the life of the clean finish and reduce future maintenance needs. Hydrophobic impregnations repel water and prevent moisture penetration, while anti-algae treatments inhibit biological regrowth. These invisible coatings preserve the natural appearance of the surface while providing long-lasting protection.

Safe Access Solutions

Façade cleaning often requires work at height. Our team is trained in safe working at height procedures and we deploy appropriate access equipment — from mobile scaffolding and cherry pickers to rope access for complex or hard-to-reach areas. All work complies with ITM regulations for working at height in Luxembourg.
