Demolition

Safe, precise demolition with complete site management — clearing the way for what comes next.

Controlled Demolition, Complete Peace of Mind

Demolition is far more than knocking things down. It demands meticulous planning, strict safety protocols and a thorough understanding of structural engineering, hazardous materials and environmental regulations. At SAM Construction, we bring discipline and precision to every demolition project in Luxembourg.

Whether it is a complete building demolition, selective interior strip-out or careful deconstruction to preserve reusable materials, our team ensures the work is done safely, efficiently and in full compliance with Luxembourg law.

Our Demolition Services

  • Complete structural demolition of residential and commercial buildings
  • Selective demolition and interior strip-outs
  • Asbestos identification, containment and removal (by certified partners)
  • Concrete cutting and controlled breaking
  • Site clearance and grading
  • Waste sorting, recycling and responsible disposal

Safety First

Every demolition project begins with a comprehensive risk assessment and method statement. We comply fully with the Inspection du Travail et des Mines (ITM) regulations on workplace safety, and our operatives hold all required certifications. Neighbouring properties are protected through vibration monitoring, dust suppression and structural shoring where necessary.

Environmental Responsibility

Luxembourg's environmental regulations require careful management of demolition waste. We sort all materials on-site, maximising recycling rates for concrete, metals, timber and other recoverable materials. Hazardous materials such as asbestos, lead paint and contaminated soils are handled according to strict protocols established by the Administration de l'Environnement.

Our waste management plans are prepared in advance and submitted as part of the permitting process, ensuring full traceability from demolition through to final disposal at licensed facilities.

Permitting & Coordination

We handle all demolition permits required by the relevant commune, including commodo/incommodo procedures where applicable. Our team coordinates with utility providers to ensure safe disconnection of gas, electricity, water and telecommunications before any work begins.
