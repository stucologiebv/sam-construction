New Construction

Full-scale residential and commercial builds, engineered to Luxembourg's highest standards from foundation to roofline.

Building From the Ground Up

At SAM Construction, new builds represent the purest expression of our craft. Every new construction project we undertake in Luxembourg is a blank canvas — an opportunity to translate architectural vision into structural reality with uncompromising precision and care.

Since 2009, we have delivered residential homes, multi-unit developments and commercial buildings across the Grand Duchy. Our team works within Luxembourg's rigorous regulatory framework, including full compliance with the Règlement Grand-Ducal on energy performance of buildings and the stringent requirements set by the Ministère de l'Énergie et de l'Aménagement du Territoire.

What We Deliver

  • Single-family homes and villas
  • Multi-unit residential developments (résidences)
  • Commercial and mixed-use buildings
  • Agricultural and industrial structures
  • Extensions and annexes to existing properties

Our Approach

Every project begins with a thorough site analysis and feasibility study. We coordinate closely with architects, engineers and the relevant communes to ensure all permits — commodo/incommodo authorisations, building permits and environmental assessments — are secured before a single foundation is poured.

We manage every phase: excavation and foundation works, structural framing in reinforced concrete or steel, roofing, envelope sealing and all finishing trades. Our in-house project management ensures that timelines are respected, budgets are maintained and quality benchmarks exceed expectations.

Energy Performance & Sustainability

All new constructions are designed to meet or surpass Luxembourg's energy efficiency class requirements. We integrate high-performance insulation systems, triple-glazed windows, heat recovery ventilation and renewable energy solutions such as heat pumps and photovoltaic panels. Our goal is to deliver buildings that are not only beautiful and functional but also sustainable and cost-efficient for decades to come.

Quality Assurance

We maintain meticulous quality control at every stage, with documented inspections and material traceability. Our partnerships with trusted Luxembourg suppliers ensure that all materials meet European CE standards and are sourced responsibly. Every project is delivered with a comprehensive handover package including technical documentation, warranty certificates and maintenance guidance.
