Civil Engineering

Roads, drainage, earthworks and infrastructure — precision-engineered groundwork that forms the backbone of every successful project.

The Foundation of Every Great Project

Civil engineering is the invisible backbone of the built environment. Roads, drainage systems, retaining walls and underground utilities — these are the essential works that make everything above ground possible. At SAM Construction, our civil engineering division delivers this critical infrastructure with the precision and reliability that Luxembourg's demanding standards require.

From private residential developments to larger-scale infrastructure projects, we bring the heavy equipment, technical expertise and regulatory knowledge needed to prepare sites and build the systems that serve them for decades.

Our Civil Engineering Services

  • Earthworks — excavation, cut and fill, site levelling and soil stabilisation
  • Road construction — access roads, private drives and parking areas
  • Drainage systems — stormwater management, soakaways and retention basins
  • Sewer connections and underground utility installation
  • Retaining walls and slope stabilisation
  • Paving — asphalt, concrete and block paving for all applications
  • Foundation works — strip, pad, raft and piled foundations

Compliance with Luxembourg Standards

Civil engineering works in Luxembourg must comply with the technical standards set by the Administration des Ponts et Chaussées, commune-specific infrastructure requirements and national environmental protection regulations. Our team is well-versed in these requirements, including the management of excavated soils under the waste classification framework and the protection of groundwater resources.

We prepare comprehensive technical submissions for infrastructure works, coordinating with utility providers (Creos, POST, water syndicates) to ensure all connections are properly planned and executed.

Equipment & Capability

Our civil engineering operations are supported by a fleet of modern plant and equipment, including excavators, loaders, compactors and grading equipment. GPS-guided earthmoving systems ensure precision grading to exact levels, reducing material waste and accelerating project timelines.

Environmental Management

Every civil engineering project includes a detailed environmental management plan. We implement erosion control measures, sediment traps and dust suppression to protect the surrounding environment during works. Excavated materials are characterised and managed according to Luxembourg's waste hierarchy — reusing on-site wherever possible, recycling where appropriate and disposing responsibly when necessary.
