Landscaping

Transforming outdoor spaces with professional landscaping — from garden design and terracing to complete exterior environments.

Crafting Outdoor Living Spaces

A beautifully designed exterior is the natural extension of a well-built home. At SAM Construction, our landscaping services complete the picture — transforming raw ground into gardens, terraces, driveways and outdoor living areas that complement the architecture and enhance quality of life.

Luxembourg's varied terrain — from the rolling hills of the Mullerthal to the flatlands of the Moselle — presents unique opportunities and challenges for landscape design. Our team understands the local climate, soil conditions and native plant palette, ensuring that every outdoor space we create is both beautiful and sustainable.

Our Landscaping Services

  • Garden design and planting schemes using native and adapted species
  • Terrace construction — natural stone, composite decking and concrete
  • Driveway and pathway installation
  • Retaining walls and decorative stonework
  • Lawn installation — seeded and turfed
  • Irrigation system design and installation
  • Outdoor lighting design and installation
  • Fencing, gates and boundary treatments

Integrated Approach

As a full-service construction company, we bring a unique advantage to landscaping projects. Our civil engineering capability means we can handle the heavy groundwork — grading, drainage, retaining structures — while our finishing trades deliver the precision details like stonework, timber structures and outdoor lighting. This integrated approach eliminates coordination issues between separate contractors and delivers a cohesive result.

Sustainability & Biodiversity

Luxembourg's commitment to biodiversity protection extends to private properties. We design landscapes that support local ecosystems, incorporating native planting, wildlife corridors and sustainable drainage solutions. Our designs comply with commune regulations regarding permeable surface ratios and green space requirements.

We also install rainwater harvesting systems, permeable paving and bioswales that manage stormwater naturally, reducing the burden on municipal drainage systems and contributing to groundwater recharge.

Year-Round Beauty

Luxembourg's distinct four seasons demand careful plant selection to ensure interest throughout the year. We select species that provide spring blossoms, summer colour, autumn foliage and winter structure, creating gardens that reward in every season. Evergreen frameworks, ornamental grasses and carefully chosen trees form the backbone of designs that look established from day one.
