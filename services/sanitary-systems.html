Energy Efficiency at the Core

Luxembourg's energy performance regulations (Règlement Grand-Ducal on energy performance) set demanding targets for heating systems, hot water production and ventilation. Our installations are designed to meet or exceed these requirements, helping achieve the energy efficiency class needed for building certification and eligibility for PRIMe House subsidies.

We specify high-efficiency components — condensing boilers with seasonal efficiencies above 95%, air-source and ground-source heat pumps with COP ratings above 4.0, and heat recovery ventilation systems that recover over 90% of exhaust air energy. These specifications translate directly into lower energy bills and reduced environmental impact for our clients.

Smart Home Integration

Modern sanitary and heating systems increasingly interface with smart home controls. We install and configure programmable thermostats, zone control systems and remote monitoring solutions that allow homeowners to manage their comfort and energy use from anywhere. Integration with KNX home automation platforms is available for clients seeking the highest level of control.

Compliance & Certification

All our installations comply with Luxembourg's technical regulations, including the prescriptions of the Administration de la Gestion de l'Eau for plumbing works and the Inspection du Travail et des Mines (ITM) for heating and gas installations. We coordinate all required inspections and provide complete technical documentation for building permit compliance and energy passport certification.

Maintenance & Aftercare

A well-maintained system lasts longer and performs better. We offer guidance on maintenance schedules and can coordinate annual servicing of heating systems, ventilation filters and plumbing inspections. Our handover documentation includes detailed system schematics, equipment manuals and maintenance checklists.
