Cookie Policy

This page describes the cookies and similar technologies used on this website, in accordance with the EU ePrivacy directive and the Luxembourg law of 30 May 2005.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. Cookies allow websites to remember information about your visit.

Cookies used on this site

Strictly necessary (no consent required)

  • cookieConsent (localStorage) — remembers your cookie preference. Duration: permanent until you clear your browser storage.
  • samLang (localStorage) — remembers your selected language. Duration: permanent until cleared.

Functional / analytics / marketing

Currently none. If we introduce analytics or marketing cookies in the future, you will be asked for consent before any non-essential cookie is set.

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts — font files served from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log the IP address of requesting browsers. No cookies are set.
  • Google Maps (embedded on the contact section) — sets cookies once you load the map. Consult Google's privacy policy.
  • WhatsApp — clicking the WhatsApp button opens wa.me which is subject to Meta's policies.

How to manage cookies

You can accept or refuse cookies through our cookie banner when first visiting the site. You can also clear cookies and site data via your browser settings at any time.
